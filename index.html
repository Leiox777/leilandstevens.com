Mission

My purpose is to help develop the concepts needed to create a specialized artificial intelligence. This artificial intelligence must be capable of general intelligence. A form of intelligence similar to humans. I understand the dangers that may come with releasing an artificial super-intelligence into the world. A machine of this ability could easily destroy life as we know it, but could just as easily build a utopia. The problem is, is that it is inevitable. No matter who you are, the day the machine is created will come. I mine as well be the one helping to push the button.
